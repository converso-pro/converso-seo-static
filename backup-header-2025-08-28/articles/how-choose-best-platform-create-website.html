⚡ Crucial Factors in Your Choice

1. Website Purpose

Clearly define: is it for scheduling services, selling products, showing portfolio, or generating leads? Each platform has a focus.

2. Real Budget

Calculate not just the monthly fee, but domain, professional email, extra plugins/apps, design, and maintenance.

3. Available Time

WordPress requires 10-20 hours monthly maintenance. Managed platforms like Converso and Wix do everything for you.

4. Future Growth

Switching platforms later is expensive and laborious. Choose thinking about where you want to be in 2-3 years.

5. SEO and Marketing

WordPress and Converso are superior for SEO. Wix has improved but still lags behind. Shopify is good for product SEO.

🚨 Warning Signs When Choosing

  • Platforms that charge commission on sales - This kills your profit margin
  • Long-term contracts - Avoid commitments longer than 1 year
  • No data export option - You become hostage to the platform
  • English-only support - Problematic when you need urgent help
  • Very limited templates - Your site will look like thousands of others
  • Mandatory separate hosting - Complicates and increases costs
