🎯 The 10 Most Effective Strategies

1

Start with Your Inner Circle

Your first client is closer than you think. List everyone you know who owns a business.

WhatsApp message template:
"Hey [Name]! How are you? 😊

I'm starting to create professional websites and remembered your [type of business].

I'm offering a special discount for my first clients. Can I show you how [business name]'s website would look?

I can create a demo in 5 minutes with no obligation. Interested?"

Conversion rate: 3 out of 10 contacts become clients

2

WhatsApp/Facebook Groups

Join local entrepreneur groups. It's a goldmine of potential clients.

  • Search: "[Your city] entrepreneurs"
  • Search: "[Your city] small business"
  • Search: "[Niche] owners" (e.g., "Restaurant owners")
  • Actively participate for 1 week before offering
  • Help first, sell later

Avoid: Joining and immediately selling. Build relationships first!

3

Irresistible First 3 Offer

Create urgency and scarcity by offering special conditions only for your first 3 clients.

Social media post:
"🚀 LIMITED OPPORTUNITY!

I'm offering 50% OFF for the FIRST 3 clients who book a professional website with me this week.

✅ Complete responsive website
✅ Online booking system
✅ WhatsApp integration
✅ 1 year of support

From $2,000 to $1,000

Spots left: 3

DM me: [your number]"
4

Portfolio of 3 Demo Websites

Create websites in high-demand niches to show what you can do in practice.

Recommended niches:

  • Barbershop: "Style Barbershop" - with online booking
  • Beauty Salon: "Beauty Center" - with work gallery
  • Restaurant: "Taste & Art" - with digital menu

Total time: 15 minutes (5 min per site)

5

Instagram: Before and After

Visual transformation posts are extremely effective for attracting clients.

Format that converts:

• Slide 1: "BEFORE" - Google search screenshot with no results
• Slide 2: "AFTER" - Professional website created
• Slide 3: Benefits (24/7 booking, more clients, etc)
• Slide 4: Special offer + CTA

Hashtags: #professionalwebsite #[yourcity]business #webdeveloper #websitedesign

6

Partner with Local Influencers

Offer free website to micro-influencers (5k-20k followers) in exchange for promotion.

Partnership proposal:
"Hi [Name]! Love your work on Instagram!

I'm a developer and I'm selecting 2 influencers from the city to create a professional website for FREE.

In exchange, I only ask for:
- 3 stories showing the website
- 1 post about the experience
- Permission to use as a success story

Interested in hearing more?"
7

Google My Business: Gold Mine

Businesses without websites on Google are your best prospects.

How to do it:

  1. Search: "[niche] near me"
  2. Filter by businesses WITHOUT website
  3. Visit in person or call
  4. Show how competitors with websites appear better
  5. Offer to create a demo on the spot

Success rate: 4 out of 10 visited close deals

8

Local Events and Trade Shows

Where entrepreneurs gather, opportunities appear.

  • Bring tablet/laptop for live demonstrations
  • Create website in front of prospect (VERY impressive)
  • Prepare cards with QR Code to your demos
  • "Today only" special offer to close on the spot

Goal: 20 contacts = 5 demos = 2 clients

9

Referral System

Turn every client into a referral machine.

Post-delivery script:
"[Name], glad you love the website!

I have a proposal: for every business owner friend you refer who closes with me, you get:

✅ $150 commission
✅ OR 3 months free maintenance

Know anyone who needs a website?"

Average result: Each client refers 2-3 new ones

10

Content that Educates and Sells

Create short videos showing the problem of not having a professional website.

Scripts that work:

• "Why 73% of barbershops lose clients" (no website)
• "I searched 10 salons on Google" (only 2 had websites)
• "I created a complete website in 5 minutes" (demonstration)
• "How much you lose without online booking"

Post on: Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts

❌ The 7 Fatal Mistakes (and How to Avoid Them)

1. Pricing too low

Charging less than $500 makes you look amateur. Your work has value!

2. Not having a portfolio

Always have 3 websites ready to show. Takes 15 minutes to create.

3. Overpromising

Deliver what Converso.pro offers. Don't invent features that don't exist.

4. Slow response time

Respond within 1 hour. Hot leads cool down fast.

5. No follow-up

70% of clients close on the 2nd or 3rd contact. Persist politely.

6. Focusing only on price

Sell results: more clients, time savings, professionalism.

7. Not asking for referrals

Every satisfied client knows 3-5 other business owners. Always ask!
