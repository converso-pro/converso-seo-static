How to Save up to 70% on Your System

🎯 Proven Tips

  1. Choose annual billing: Save 20-30% by paying annually vs monthly
  2. Negotiate at renewal: After 6 months, ask for 30-50% discount by threatening to cancel
  3. Start with basics: Don't pay for features you don't use yet
  4. Compare hidden fees: Add ALL fees, not just monthly cost
  5. Test free trials: Use 2-3 systems before deciding
  6. Request custom demo: Ensure it meets your specific needs
  7. Avoid long contracts: Maximum 12 months to maintain flexibility

⚠️ Traps to Avoid

  • ❌ "Free" systems with high fees: 3% of $10k = $300/month!
  • ❌ Contracts with cancellation fees: Some charge 6 months to cancel
  • ❌ Plans with low limits: "Unlimited" that's actually 1,000/month
  • ❌ Expensive setup without guarantee: Pay implementation only after it works
  • ❌ Locked features: Basic functions only in premium plan
