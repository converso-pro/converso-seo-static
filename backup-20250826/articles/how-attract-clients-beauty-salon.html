üéØ 10 Strategies to Fill Your Appointment Book

1

Instagram That Sells

87% of clients choose salons through Instagram. Transform your profile into a client-attracting machine.

How to implement:

  • Before/after photos every day
  • Stories showing real-time transformations
  • Reels with beauty trends
  • Bio with direct booking link
2

First Visit Promotion

New clients need incentive. An irresistible offer breaks the initial barrier.

How to implement:

  • 30% OFF on first visit
  • Free hair treatment with first cut
  • Welcome kit with products
  • Free hair analysis consultation
3

Google My Business

"Salon near me" is searched 50,000x/month. Appear at the top of local searches.

How to implement:

  • 100% complete profile with photos
  • Always updated business hours
  • Respond to all reviews within 24h
  • Post updates weekly
4

Strategic Partnerships

Complementary businesses are an infinite source of referrals. One partnership = 20-30 clients/month.

How to implement:

  • Women's gym: post-workout beauty day
  • Clothing store: free makeup with purchases
  • Photographer: photoshoot + styling packages
  • Events: booth at bridal fairs
5

24/7 Booking

73% of clients want to book outside business hours. Don't lose clients while you sleep.

How to implement:

  • Website with online booking
  • Automated WhatsApp Business
  • Instant SMS confirmation
  • Choice of professional and service
6

Transformation Day

Themed events attract groups and create buzz. One event = 50+ new clients.

How to implement:

  • Monthly "Transformation Friday"
  • Free image consultation
  • Drinks and snacks at the salon
  • Exclusive raffles and gifts
7

Local Influencers

Micro-influencers (5-20k) have 7x more engagement. The right partnership = 100+ clients.

How to implement:

  • Trade with local bloggers
  • Day use for content creation
  • Exclusive discount code
  • Stories and posts tagging salon
8

Irresistible Packages

Clients spend 2.5x more on packages. Sell complete experiences, not isolated services.

How to implement:

  • "Bride's Day": hair + makeup + nails
  • "Total Renewal": 3 services for price of 2
  • "VIP Club": monthly unlimited services
  • "Mother & Daughter": progressive discount
9

WhatsApp Marketing

98% of messages are read within 3 minutes. Direct channel with your clients.

How to implement:

  • Weekly broadcast list
  • Open schedule with last-minute spots
  • Personalized care tips
  • Exclusive offers for VIP list
10

5-Star Experience

Clients pay 86% more for memorable experiences. Be unforgettable.

How to implement:

  • Gourmet coffee and flavored waters
  • Relaxing massage during treatments
  • Personalized Spotify playlist
  • Birthday surprise gift
