Frequently Asked Questions

How long for the salon to be profitable?

A well-located and managed salon can start being profitable between 6-18 months. In the first 6 months, focus on building clientele. Real profit comes with loyal customers.

Better to hire experienced professionals or train?

The ideal is a mix: 1-2 experienced professionals to ensure quality and attract clients, and professionals in training for lower costs. Always invest in continuous training.

How to set service prices?

Research local competition, calculate your costs (product + labor + overhead) and add 40-60% margin. Adjust according to demand and salon positioning.

Do I need experience in the industry to open a salon?

It's not mandatory to be a stylist to own a salon, but it's essential to understand the business. Many owners focus on management and hire qualified technical professionals.
