‚ùì Common Digital Marketing Questions

How much should I spend on digital marketing?

Start with 5-10% of revenue. For most barbershops, that's $500-1,500/month. But with the right tools like Converso, you can do most marketing for just $18-48/month plus your time investment.

How long before I see results?

Website and booking system: immediate results. Social media: 30-60 days for momentum. SEO: 3-6 months for rankings. Email marketing: results from day one. Most shops see 20-30% growth in the first 90 days.

Do I need to be on every platform?

No! Start with Google Business Profile and Instagram. Add TikTok if you can create video content. Facebook is optional unless your clients are 40+. Quality beats quantity - do fewer platforms well.

What if I'm not tech-savvy?

Modern tools are designed for busy barbers, not tech experts. Platforms like Converso handle the technical stuff - you just upload photos and write simple captions. Most barbers master it in a week.

How do I compete with big chain shops?

Your advantage is personality and personal service. Show your unique style, build real relationships, and provide experiences chains can't match. Digital marketing amplifies what makes you special.
