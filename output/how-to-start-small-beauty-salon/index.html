Frequently Asked Questions

Is it possible to open a salon with just $400?

Yes! Starting at home with basic used equipment. You need: table, chair, mirror, scissor kit, dryer and basic products. Business registration is often free for sole proprietors.

How long does it take to get ROI?

With a loyal clientele and low costs (home salon), ROI can happen in 6-12 months. In a rented space, it normally takes 12-24 months depending on location and marketing.

Do I need cosmetology school to open a salon?

Requirements vary by state. Many states require a cosmetology license to operate, but some allow you to hire licensed stylists while managing the business.

How to get first clients?

Start with family and friends, ask for referrals, use social media to show your work, offer grand opening specials and have a professional booking system like Converso.
