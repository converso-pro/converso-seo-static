❓ Common Questions from Clark County Barbers

Is it really free forever?

Yes! You get 10 bookings per month absolutely free, forever. No credit card required, no trial period. Many small shops in Clark County run their entire business on the free plan.

What happens after 10 bookings?

You can still accept walk-ins and phone bookings. To accept more online bookings, upgrade to Plus ($18/mo) for 500 bookings or Pro ($48/mo) for unlimited. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime.

Do I need technical skills?

Not at all! If you can use Instagram or Facebook, you can use our app. Everything is drag-and-drop simple. Most Clark County barbers are up and running in 5 minutes.

Can I accept walk-ins?

Absolutely! Our digital queue system is perfect for Vegas's busy walk-in culture. Clients can join the queue from their phone and get real-time updates on wait times.

What about payment processing?

We integrate with Stripe for secure payments. You keep 100% of your service fees - we never charge booking commissions. Stripe's standard 2.9% + 30¢ processing fee applies.

How is this different from other apps?

Unlike Square, Acuity, or Booksy, we charge zero booking fees and include a professional website. Our app is designed specifically for barbershops, not generic businesses. Plus, we're truly free to start!
