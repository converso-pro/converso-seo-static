Frequently Asked Questions About Opening a Store

Is it worth opening a physical store in 2025?

It depends on your niche. For products that need to be touched/tried (clothing, shoes), the hybrid model (online + showroom) has shown better results. 100% physical stores face 10x higher costs and 40% lower margins than online stores.

Can I start without inventory?

Yes! With dropshipping or made-to-order products, you can start with zero inventory. Converso allows you to create a digital catalog, receive orders and payments before buying the products, reducing your financial risk.

How long until I make a profit?

Well-structured online stores can generate profits in 2-3 months. Physical stores take 8-12 months on average. With Converso, you reduce the break-even point by 70% due to minimal operating costs.

Do I need a business license to sell online?

In the United States, you need a Business License (cost: $50-400 depending on state) and Tax ID. It's recommended to issue invoices and get better payment rates. Converso works for both individuals and established companies.
