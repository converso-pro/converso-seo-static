Frequently Asked Questions

Do customers need to download an app?

No! Digital Queue works entirely through web browsers. Customers simply scan your QR code and join the queue instantly. The entire process takes less than 30 seconds and works on any smartphone.

How accurate are the wait time estimates?

Very accurate. The system learns your shop's pace over time, tracking how long each barber takes for different services. Most shops see 90%+ accuracy within the first week. You can also manually adjust estimates based on service complexity.

What if someone doesn't have a smartphone?

No problem! Your staff can add walk-ins manually through the dashboard in seconds. You can also display the queue on a TV screen so everyone can see their position. The system is flexible for all customer types.

Can we still take appointments alongside the queue?

Absolutely! Digital Queue integrates seamlessly with Converso's appointment booking system. The queue automatically adjusts wait times based on upcoming appointments. You get the best of both worlds - organized appointments and efficient walk-in management.

How do SMS notifications work?

Customers receive automated texts when they're 5-10 minutes away from their turn. You can customize the timing and message. Standard SMS rates apply (about $0.01 per message), but the ROI is massive - one saved customer covers hundreds of texts.

What happens during internet outages?

The system has offline capabilities. Your dashboard continues working locally, and syncs when connection returns. Customers might not be able to join remotely during an outage, but you can still add them manually. We've built it to be barbershop-reliable.
