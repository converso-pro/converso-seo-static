Frequently Asked Questions

Do clients need to download an app?

No! Everything works in their web browser. They can bookmark your page or save it to their home screen if they want quick access. Zero friction for clients.

Can I still accept walk-ins?

Absolutely! Our Digital Queue feature lets walk-ins join a virtual line. They get a text when their turn is near. Or just keep slots open for walk-ins - you control everything.

What about last-minute cancellations?

Set your cancellation policy (like 2-hour notice). System automatically opens the slot and can notify waitlisted clients. You can also blast available slots to fill them quickly.

How do clients pay?

You choose: pay at shop, pay when booking, or deposit required. If you enable payments, money goes directly to your Stripe account (minus standard 2.9% processing fee).

What if I want to stop using it?

No contracts, cancel anytime. Export all your client data with one click. But with 94% of shops staying long-term, you'll likely never want to go back to the old way.
