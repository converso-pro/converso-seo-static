Frequently Asked Questions

Is it possible to make money with $20?

Yes! With dropshipping or digital products you can start with $20-40 and generate sales in the first month. The key is choosing products with good margin and focusing on initial organic marketing.

How long to get the first sales?

With focused strategy, first sales can come in 7-15 days. Digital products and dropshipping tend to convert faster than physical products with inventory.

What's the best free platform?

Converso offers complete e-commerce without monthly fees. For beginners, it's also worth using Instagram Shopping + WhatsApp Business as complement.

How to choose the right products?

Focus on products you know, with minimum 100% margin, adequate weight/size for shipping, and proven demand (use Google Trends and social media).
