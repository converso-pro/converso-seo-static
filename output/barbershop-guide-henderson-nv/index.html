Nevada State Requirements for Barbershops

🏛️ State Board of Cosmetology Requirements

Establishment License

  • Application fee: $140
  • Annual renewal: $125
  • Must pass inspection before opening
  • Display license prominently

Sanitation Requirements

  • Wet sanitizer for each station
  • UV cabinet or dry sanitizer
  • Covered waste receptacles
  • Proper ventilation system

Physical Space Requirements

  • Minimum 100 sq ft per barber
  • Separate restroom facilities
  • Hot and cold running water
  • Adequate lighting (50 foot-candles)

Record Keeping

  • Client appointment records
  • Employee licenses on file
  • Chemical usage logs
  • Digital records accepted (use booking system)

Frequently Asked Questions - Henderson Barbershops

Do I need a barber license to own a barbershop?

No, you don't need to be a licensed barber to own a barbershop in Nevada. However, all barbers working in your shop must have valid Nevada barber licenses. You'll need business licenses and an establishment license from the State Board.

What's the best area in Henderson for a new barbershop?

Green Valley and Seven Hills offer affluent clientele but higher rent. Downtown Henderson provides great value with growing foot traffic. Anthem is emerging with less competition. Consider your target market and budget when choosing.

How much should I charge for services?

Henderson average: Haircuts $30-45, Beard trim $20-30, Kids cuts $20-25. Premium areas like Seven Hills can charge 20-30% more. Start competitive and increase as you build reputation.

Should I rent chairs or hire employees?

Both work in Henderson. Chair rental ($200-400/week) gives you steady income with less management. Employees (commission 50-60%) give you more control over quality and scheduling. Many shops use a hybrid model.

How long until I'm profitable?

With proper planning and marketing, most Henderson barbershops break even in 3-4 months and become profitable by month 6. Having 6 months of operating capital gives you breathing room to build clientele.

What technology is essential from day one?

Online booking is non-negotiable - 78% of clients expect it. Start with Converso's free plan (10 bookings/month) and a basic POS system. Add marketing tools and loyalty programs as you grow.
