Frequently Asked Questions - Henderson

How much does it cost to start?

Absolutely free! Start with 10 bookings per month at no cost. Upgrade to Plus ($18/month) or Pro ($48/month) as you grow. No setup fees, no contracts.

Does it work with Nevada regulations?

Yes! Converso is fully compliant with Nevada State Board of Cosmetology requirements. We maintain digital records, support license tracking, and provide easy exports for inspections.

Can I accept payments online?

Yes! Connect your Stripe account to accept secure card payments. This helps reduce no-shows by up to 85% when clients prepay for appointments.

What languages are supported?

English, Spanish, and Portuguese. Perfect for Henderson's diverse community. Your clients can book in their preferred language, and you manage everything in yours.

How do I handle walk-ins?

Our Digital Queue app lets walk-ins check in via QR code. They get real-time updates on wait times via SMS. You manage everything from your dashboard.

Is there local support?

Yes! We have a dedicated Nevada team available via WhatsApp, email, and phone. Support in all three languages, and we understand the local market.
