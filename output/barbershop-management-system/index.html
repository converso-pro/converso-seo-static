Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Will my barbers actually use this system?

Yes! The interface is designed specifically for barbers, not tech people. Most barbers master it in 30 minutes. Plus, when they see their tips and commissions in real-time, adoption is instant. We have 94% daily active usage across all shops.

Can I import my existing client database?

Absolutely. We import from any format - Excel, CSV, even handwritten lists. Our migration team handles everything, including de-duplication and data cleaning. Most imports complete in under 2 hours with zero data loss.

What if I have multiple locations?

Perfect! Our multi-location dashboard lets you manage all shops from one screen. See combined analytics, move staff between locations, and maintain consistent pricing. Each location can have unique settings while sharing client databases.

How does inventory tracking actually work?

Link products to services (e.g., 2oz gel per haircut). The system automatically deducts inventory with each service. When supplies hit your reorder point, you get alerts. Approve orders with one click. Track costs per service for true profitability.

Is my financial data secure?

Bank-level secure. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, are PCI compliant, and undergo annual security audits. Your data is backed up hourly to multiple locations. You own your data and can export it anytime. We've never had a security breach in 8 years.
