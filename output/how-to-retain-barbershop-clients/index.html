Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Converso Loyalty App really free?

Yes! The loyalty program is available free for all users. On Plus/Pro plans you get additional features like Client Portal where clients can see their points and progress in real-time.

How are points calculated?

You define the rule. For example: $1 = 1 point. When a client completes a $45 service, they automatically earn 45 points. You can also use a visit-based system (each haircut = 1 visit).

Do clients need to download an app?

No! Clients access the Client Portal directly through their mobile browser. They receive a unique link via SMS/WhatsApp and can view their points, history, and redeem rewards without installing anything.

Can I manage points manually?

Yes! Besides automatic accumulation, you can add or remove points manually. Useful for special promotions, corrections, or extra bonuses.

How do rewards work?

You generate unique reward codes that clients apply on their next appointment. Can be percentage discount (15% off), fixed value ($10 off), or free service.
