Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a license to work as a hairstylist?

Yes, all US states require a license. You must complete the required hours (500-1,600 depending on state) and pass the state board exam.

How long to recover my investment?

Online course: 1-2 months. Community College: 3-4 months. Premium school: 6-8 months. Depends on your dedication and client building.

Is online course worth it?

Yes! Perfect for working people or tight budgets. Learn at your pace and save up to 80%. Supplement with hands-on workshops later.

What's the best school for beginners?

Community College offers the best value: accredited certificate, financial aid, payment plans. Paul Mitchell is excellent for networking and prestige.

Can I work while studying?

Yes! Many students work as salon assistants while studying. You gain experience, networking, and earn a salary.
