Common Questions About Shopify Costs

Can I really create a Shopify store without a developer?

Yes, but it will be very basic. For a professional store with custom features, you'll need to hire someone or spend weeks learning. With Converso, everything is ready in 5 minutes.

Why does Shopify charge transaction fees?

Shopify takes 2.4-2.9% of every sale plus 30¢. If you use external payment processors, they charge an additional 2%. This is how they make most of their money - from YOUR sales.

What happens if I want to leave Shopify?

Migration is extremely difficult and expensive. You'll need to rebuild everything from scratch. Many businesses feel trapped. With Converso, you can export your data anytime.

Do I really need all those Shopify apps?

Yes, for basic features like email marketing, reviews, SEO, multi-language, you'll need 5-10 apps minimum. Each costs $10-100/month. Converso includes everything.

Is Converso really easier than Shopify?
