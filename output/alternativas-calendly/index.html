Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a true free Calendly alternative?

Yes! Converso offers 10 free bookings/month forever, SimplyBook.me provides 50 bookings/month, and Setmore has unlimited bookings for basic use. Cal.com is free if you self-host.

Which alternative has the best mobile app?

Calendly, Acuity, and SimplyBook.me have dedicated iOS/Android apps. Converso uses a Progressive Web App that works perfectly on mobile. Cal.com is web-only.

Can I accept payments through these platforms?

Yes! Converso, Acuity, Cal.com, and SimplyBook.me all support direct payment processing. Calendly only supports PayPal payments on higher plans.

Which is best for international businesses?

SimplyBook.me supports 30+ languages, while Converso offers English, Spanish, and Portuguese with multi-currency support. Both are excellent for international use.

Do these alternatives integrate with my calendar?

All major alternatives sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, and iCal. Some offer deeper integrations with CRM and marketing tools.

Which platform is easiest to set up?

SimplyBook.me and Converso both offer 5-minute setup with templates. Calendly is also quick but limited. Cal.com requires more technical knowledge.

💡 Pro Tip: Start with free plans to test multiple platforms. Most businesses find they need more than just scheduling - consider platforms like Converso that include marketing, payments, and client management tools to avoid needing multiple subscriptions.
