Addressing Your Professional Concerns

"What about client confidentiality?"

Privacy is our priority. All communications are encrypted, reminders can be customized to be discreet, and client data is securely stored. We understand the unique requirements of mental health practices.

"Will online booking reduce the therapeutic relationship?"

Actually, it enhances it. Clients appreciate the respect for their time and the professional boundaries. You'll spend session time on therapy, not scheduling logistics.

"What if I need to manage crisis situations?"

Include crisis resources on your booking page, set emergency appointment slots, and maintain control over your availability. The system supports your clinical judgment.

"How does this work with insurance?"

Collect insurance information during booking, generate superbills easily, and track which clients use insurance vs. private pay. Simplifies your billing process significantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Converso cost for therapists?

Start free with 10 sessions/month to test the system. Plus plan ($18/month) includes 500 bookings - perfect for most practices. Pro plan ($48/month) offers unlimited bookings and priority support. No booking fees ever.

Can I customize intake forms?

Yes! Create custom intake forms to collect necessary information before the first session. Forms are securely stored and can include consent documents, insurance details, and clinical questionnaires.

How do cancellations work?

Set your cancellation policy (24, 48, 72 hours), and the system enforces it automatically. Clients can reschedule within your parameters, and you maintain full control over exceptions.

Is this HIPAA compliant?

While Converso focuses on appointment scheduling (not clinical records), we maintain high security standards with encryption and privacy protections suitable for mental health practices.

Can I block time for notes and self-care?

Absolutely! Set buffer times between appointments, block lunch hours, and schedule admin time. The system respects your boundaries and prevents overbooking.

What makes this different from therapy-specific EMRs?

Simplicity and focus. While EMRs cost $200+ monthly and include features you may not need, Converso perfects scheduling at a fraction of the cost. Many therapists use us alongside their preferred notes system.
