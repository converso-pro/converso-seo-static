Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a physical store to resell iPhones?

No! 78% of resellers start 100% online. With a professional website and digital marketing strategies, you can sell nationwide. Physical store only makes sense after reaching 30+ monthly sales consistently.

What's the best iPhone model to start reselling?

iPhone 13 and 14 offer the best value for beginners. High demand, good margins ($150-250/unit), and quick turnover. iPhone SE is great for customers wanting to enter the Apple ecosystem affordably.

How do I compete with big retailers?

Focus on: personalized service, home delivery/setup, flexible payment terms, exclusive bonuses (cases, chargers), superior after-sales support. Small resellers have agility that big chains can't match.

Is it safe to buy iPhones from auctions?

Official liquidation auctions can have great prices but require knowledge: verify IMEI status, actual condition, calculate all costs including shipping and fees. For beginners, better to start with established distributors until you gain experience.

How long until I see consistent profits?

With dedication and proper strategy: months 1-2 cover costs, months 3-4 see real profit, month 6 reach $4,000+/month. Secret is reinvesting initial profits to increase inventory and variety. Patience and consistency are key.
