Hair salons need multi-stylist scheduling, complex service management (like color + cut combinations), variable timing for different services, visual portfolio integration, and loyalty program features to encourage repeat visits. Generic booking systems often lack these specialized features.

How much can booking fees cost my hair salon annually?

With average hair salon services costing $58 and transaction fees ranging from 2.5-2.9%, a salon processing 400 monthly appointments pays $6,700-7,800 annually in booking fees. Converso's zero-fee model saves this entire amount while providing superior features.

Can I manage multiple stylists with different specialties?

Yes. Converso supports unlimited staff members with individual calendars, service specializations, skill levels, and pricing. You can assign specific services to certain stylists (like color specialists) and set different rates for junior vs senior stylists.

How does the loyalty program work for hair salons?

Converso's built-in loyalty program tracks client visits and spending, automatically applying rewards for repeat customers. You can set custom rewards like "10th haircut free" or "$20 off after spending $300." This increases retention by an average of 45% for hair salons.

Converso handles long-duration and multi-step services perfectly. You can book 3-4 hour color sessions, block time for consultations, and even split complex services across multiple appointments. Buffer times between services ensure adequate processing time.

How quickly will I see results after implementing a professional booking system?
