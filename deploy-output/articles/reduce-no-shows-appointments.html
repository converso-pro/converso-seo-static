7 Proven Strategies to Reduce No-Shows

Implemented by salons, clinics, and professionals with immediate results

📱

1. Automated SMS & WhatsApp Reminders

Send automatic reminders 24h and 2h before appointments. Studies show that SMS has 98% open rate, far superior to email (22%) or phone calls (40%).

Reduces no-shows by 45%
💳

2. Prepayment or Deposit

Request 30-50% advance payment of service value. Clients who pay in advance are 87% less likely to miss their appointment.

Reduces no-shows by 65%
✅

3. Mandatory Confirmation

Require confirmation 24h before or slot is automatically released. System sends message and waits for "YES" response to maintain booking.

Reduces no-shows by 38%
📋

4. Smart Waitlist

When someone cancels or doesn't confirm, the system automatically notifies waitlisted clients, filling vacant slots within minutes.

Recovers 72% of slots
⚠️

5. No-Show Fee Policy

Charge $50 fee or 30% of service value for no-shows without notice. Make policy clear at booking time and first reminder.

Reduces no-shows by 55%
🎁

6. Loyalty Program

Award points for attendance and penalize no-shows. After 10 visits, client gets discount. One no-show resets half the points.

Reduces no-shows by 28%
📊

7. History & Auto-Blocking

System tracks no-shows and automatically blocks after 3 occurrences. Client must call to unblock, creating educational barrier.

Reduces repeat offenses by 82%
