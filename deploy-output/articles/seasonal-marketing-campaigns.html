6-Week Campaign Implementation Timeline

6

Week 6 Before: Strategic Planning

Analyze previous year's data, set campaign goals, define target segments, and create initial campaign concept. Research competitor campaigns and industry trends.

5

Week 5 Before: Creative Development

Design campaign visuals, write copy variations, create landing pages, and develop email templates. Ensure brand consistency across all materials.

4

Week 4 Before: Content Production

Produce videos, photoshoots, blog posts, and social media content. Create gift guides, product bundles, and promotional materials.

3

Week 3 Before: Technical Setup

Configure email automation, set up tracking pixels, test checkout flow, and prepare inventory. Create promotional codes and update pricing.

2

Week 2 Before: Testing & Optimization

A/B test email subject lines, preview campaigns internally, optimize for mobile, and conduct final quality checks. Train customer service team.

1

Week 1 Before: Pre-Launch & Teasers

Send teaser emails, post countdown content on social media, notify VIP customers, and finalize all campaign elements. Monitor early engagement.

🚀

Launch Week: Execute & Monitor

Launch campaign across all channels, monitor performance in real-time, adjust budgets based on results, and respond to customer inquiries promptly.

Seasonal Marketing Best Practices

📊

Data-Driven Timing

Use historical data to identify optimal send times. Most successful seasonal campaigns launch Tuesday-Thursday at 10 AM or 2 PM local time.

🎨

Seasonal Design Language

Update visual elements to match seasonal themes while maintaining brand consistency. Use colors, icons, and imagery that evoke the season.

📧

Multi-Touch Campaigns

Plan 5-7 touchpoints throughout the campaign: announcement, reminder, last chance, and follow-up. Vary messaging to maintain interest.

🎯

Segment & Personalize

Tailor campaigns to customer segments based on purchase history, location, and preferences. Personalized campaigns see 4x higher engagement.

📱

Mobile-First Approach

65% of seasonal shopping happens on mobile. Ensure all campaign elements are optimized for small screens and fast loading.

🔄

Post-Campaign Analysis

Document what worked and what didn't. Create a playbook for next year with specific metrics, insights, and improvement areas.
