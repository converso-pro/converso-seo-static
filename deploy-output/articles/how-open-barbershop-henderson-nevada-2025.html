❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a licensed barber to open a barbershop?

Not necessarily. You can be the owner/manager, but you must hire licensed barbers to perform services. Each barber must have a valid Nevada license.

What's the best time to open?

January-March is ideal for preparation, aiming to open in April-May when demand increases. Avoid opening in December due to holidays.

How long until break-even?

With good location and marketing, expect 3-6 months. Henderson barbershops typically reach profitability faster than the national average due to high purchasing power.

Is it worth competing with chain barbershops?

Yes! 68% of customers prefer local barbershops for the personalized experience. Focus on quality, atmosphere, and relationships.
