❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is it better to work as an employee or self-employed?

It depends on your profile. Employment offers stability, benefits, and fixed salary of $35,000-65,000. Self-employment has potential to earn 2-3x more ($55,000-85,000), but requires discipline for management and client acquisition. Many technicians start as employees to gain experience and then migrate to self-employment.

How long does it take to become a specialized technician?

With dedication, in 6-12 months you master basic repairs. To become a specialist in boards and microsoldering, 2-3 years of practice are necessary. Courses accelerate learning: basic ($500-1,000), advanced ($2,000-3,000).

What's the initial investment to set up a repair shop?

Minimum investment: $5,000-8,000 (basic tools, initial stock, simple location). Ideal investment: $15,000-25,000 (includes professional machines, complete stock, initial marketing). With Converso.pro you save $3,000-5,000 on website and systems.

How to get the first clients?

1) Create professional profiles on Google My Business and social media. 2) Offer discount for first clients in exchange for reviews. 3) Partner with phone stores. 4) Use Converso.pro to have professional website and appear on Google. 5) Ask for referrals from friends and family.
