⚠️ Common Beginner Developer Mistakes

1. Charging too little: Professional websites are worth at least $500. Don't undervalue your work.
2. Over-promising: Use only the features already available on the platform. Don't promise complex customizations.
3. Not charging monthly fees: Always include the monthly plan in the quote. It's your guaranteed recurring revenue.
4. Doing everything alone: When you reach 20 clients, hire an assistant for operational tasks.
5. Not specializing: Choose a niche and become a reference in it. It's easier to sell when you're an expert.
