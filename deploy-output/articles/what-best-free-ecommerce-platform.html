Developer FAQ

Yes! You can edit CSS, add custom JavaScript, and choose from various templates. For advanced customizations, we offer enterprise plans with full HTML/CSS access.

Complete REST API to create/manage stores, products, orders, customers and bookings. Full documentation with examples in Python, PHP, Node.js and curl.

Can I migrate data from other platforms?

Yes! We offer migration scripts for WooCommerce, Shopify, PrestaShop and generic CSV. Our team assists with migration for partners.

What's the difference between using Converso vs building from scratch?

Converso saves 6-12 months of development. You get: payments, inventory, SEO, security, hosting, CDN, backup, support - all ready and tested by thousands of stores.

Partners have access to our L2 support. For basic questions, we provide white-label materials. For technical issues, our team resolves via API or dashboard.
