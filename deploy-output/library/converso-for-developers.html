Frequently Asked Questions about the Program

Do I need to pay anything to participate?

No! The program is 100% free. You create sites in draft mode at no cost and only earn commissions when you transfer to paying clients.

How do I receive commissions?

Automatic monthly payment via bank transfer or PayPal. You receive a detailed report of all your active sites and values.

What if the client cancels?

You receive proportionally to the months the client stayed active. That's why we focus on creating quality sites that generate results - satisfied clients don't cancel!

Can I use my own brand?

Yes! Premium developers have white label option. The client doesn't even need to know they're using Converso - it appears as your own system.

How long does approval take?

We review applications within 48 business hours. If approved, you can start creating sites immediately.
