‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Which strategy gives fastest results?

WhatsApp Business and referral programs can generate results in 1-2 weeks. Google Ads is also fast but requires higher investment.

Do I need to use all 12 strategies?

No. Start with 3-4 strategies that make most sense for your business. Small businesses: focus on WhatsApp, referrals and reviews. Larger companies: add email marketing and Google Ads.

What's the minimum investment to start?

$150-300/month is enough to implement 3-4 basic strategies. As results appear, reinvest profits to scale.

How to measure ROI of each strategy?

Use UTMs to track lead sources, implement conversion pixels and maintain detailed spreadsheets. Tools like Google Analytics and CRM are essential.

Do free strategies work?

Yes, but with limitations. Organic social media, basic SEO and networking can generate results, but growth is slower. Investment accelerates results.

How to choose the right strategies?

Analyze where your target audience is, available budget and team expertise. B2B: LinkedIn and email. B2C: Instagram and WhatsApp. E-commerce: Google Ads and influencers.
