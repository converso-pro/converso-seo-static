üéØ 7 Essential Marketing Automations for Small Business

1

Welcome Email Series

Automatically greet new subscribers with a 3-email sequence introducing your business, sharing your story, and offering a first-time discount.

  • Email 1: Welcome & thank you (immediate)
  • Email 2: Your story & values (Day 2)
  • Email 3: Special offer (Day 5)
2

Appointment Reminders

Reduce no-shows by 80% with automated SMS and email reminders sent 24 hours and 2 hours before appointments.

  • 24-hour reminder with confirmation link
  • 2-hour reminder with location details
  • Post-appointment follow-up
3

Birthday & Anniversary Campaigns

Build loyalty by automatically sending personalized offers on customer birthdays and service anniversaries.

  • Birthday discount (20% off)
  • Anniversary thank you
  • VIP status updates
4

Review Request Automation

Automatically request reviews 2 hours after service completion when satisfaction is highest.

  • Thank you message
  • Direct review links
  • Incentive for feedback
5

Win-Back Campaigns

Re-engage customers who haven't visited in 60+ days with special "we miss you" offers.

  • 60-day check-in
  • 90-day special offer
  • 120-day final attempt
6

Abandoned Cart Recovery

Recover 30% of lost sales by automatically following up when customers leave items in their cart.

  • 1-hour gentle reminder
  • 24-hour with discount
  • 72-hour last chance
7

Customer Education Series

Build trust by automatically sending helpful tips and tutorials based on purchased services.

  • Service care instructions
  • Pro tips & tricks
  • Seasonal recommendations
