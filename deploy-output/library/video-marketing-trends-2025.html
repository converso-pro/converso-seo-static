Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need to start video marketing?

Just a smartphone! Modern phones shoot excellent video. Add a $20 tripod and use natural window light. As you grow, invest in a microphone ($50) for better audio quality.

How long should my marketing videos be?

Platform-dependent: TikTok/Reels (15-60 seconds), YouTube Shorts (under 60 seconds), Instagram feed (up to 90 seconds), YouTube main (2-10 minutes), LinkedIn (30 seconds to 5 minutes).

How often should I post video content?

Consistency beats frequency. Start with 1-2 videos per week and maintain that schedule. Daily posting works for short-form content if you can sustain it without sacrificing quality.

What types of videos work best for small businesses?

Behind-the-scenes content, customer testimonials, product demos, educational tutorials, and team introductions. Authentic, helpful content outperforms polished commercials.

How do I measure video marketing success?

Track views, watch time, engagement rate, click-through rate, and conversions. Use platform analytics and UTM codes to connect video views to actual sales and bookings.
