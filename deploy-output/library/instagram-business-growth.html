Frequently Asked Questions

How many hashtags should I use on Instagram?

Use 20-30 hashtags per post for maximum reach. Mix popular hashtags (1M+ posts) with niche ones (10K-100K posts) relevant to your business and location.

What's the best time to post on Instagram?

Generally, 11 AM - 1 PM and 7 PM - 9 PM work best, but check your Instagram Insights to see when your specific audience is most active.

How often should I post on Instagram for business?

Post to your feed 4-7 times per week, Stories 1-2 times daily, and Reels 3-4 times per week for optimal engagement without overwhelming followers.

Should I use Instagram ads for my small business?

Yes, even with a small budget ($5-10/day), Instagram ads can effectively reach local customers. Start by promoting your best-performing posts to a targeted local audience.
