💡 7 Tips from Top-Earning Developers

1. Specialize in One Niche

Focus on barbershops OR salons OR restaurants. When you master a niche, you create sites faster, charge more, and become the reference. John from LA focuses only on barbershops and earns $8k/month.

2. Create a Killer Portfolio

Make 3 demo sites in your niche (use fictional names). Show on tablet during meetings. Client seeing = client buying. Invest 2 hours in this and triple your sales.

3. In-Person Prospecting Works

Visit 10 businesses per day. Show website on tablet. 3 out of 10 will be interested, 1 will close. In 20 business days = 20 new clients. Much better than Instagram ads.

4. Offer Packages, Not Hours

"Complete Website for $600" sells more than "$75/hour". Client wants results, not hours. Include everything: site, hosting, SSL, 3 months support.

5. Automate Post-Sales

Use templates for everything: proposals, contracts, welcome emails, manuals. Set automatic reminders for monthly charges. Save 10h/week.

6. Upsell is Your Friend

Happy client with the site? Offer: email marketing (+$200), social media management (+$150/month), professional photos (+$100). Increase average ticket by 50%.

7. Build Relationships

Send message on business anniversary. Share useful tips. Be their "tech guy". Happy client refers 3 new clients per year. Cultivate this.
