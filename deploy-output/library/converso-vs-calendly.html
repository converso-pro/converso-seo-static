Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Calendly without problems?

Yes, but you might face challenges: mostly English interface, USD pricing, limited support options, and fewer payment method options in some regions.

Does Converso have as many integrations as Calendly?

Not yet, but it has the essential integrations (Google Calendar, Outlook, Zoom, Google Meet) and is growing rapidly. For most professionals, it's sufficient.

Which is easier to set up?

Converso is easier for most users because of its intuitive interface and responsive support that can help with any difficulties.

Can I have my own domain?

With Converso Plus yes (yoursite.com). With Calendly you'll always have calendly.com/yourname.

Which has better ROI for service professionals?

Converso offers better ROI because: more generous free plan, transparent pricing, SMS included, and professional website included. The investment pays for itself quickly.
