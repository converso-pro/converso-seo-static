🎯 The 7 Steps to Your First Online Sale

1

Create Your Online Store

Time needed: 5 minutes
Cost: Free on Converso.pro

Choose a platform that doesn't require technical knowledge. With Converso.pro, you create your store in 5 minutes: choose your name, select your niche (fashion, electronics, beauty, etc.) and you're done! Your store is online with a professional address.

2

Add Your Products

Golden tip: Photos sell!

• Use natural light for photography
• Show product from multiple angles
• Write detailed descriptions
• Include measurements and specifications
• Be honest about product condition

3

Set Up Payment Methods

Essential options:

• Credit/Debit cards
• PayPal (instant)
• Bank transfer
• Buy now, pay later options

Converso.pro comes with all these options configured, you just need to connect your bank account.

4

Promote Your Store

Start with the basics:

• Share on WhatsApp (family and friends)
• Post on Instagram and Facebook
• Join Facebook groups in your niche
• Use WhatsApp status
• Ask friends to share

5

Process the Order

When your first sale arrives:

• Confirm payment
• Contact the customer
• Confirm delivery address
• Inform shipping deadline
• Maintain clear communication

6

Pack with Care

First impression matters:

• Use appropriate packaging
• Protect the product well
• Include invoice (or receipt)
• Add a thank you card
• Make presentation count

7

Ship and Track

Shipping options:

• Post office (cheapest)
• Courier services
• Express delivery
• Personal delivery (same city)

Always provide tracking number!

💡 5 Golden Tips for Beginners

📸

Invest in Good Photos

Products with professional photos sell 3x more. Use natural light, white background and show important details.

💬

Respond Quickly

Customers expect responses in minutes. Set up mobile notifications to not miss sales.

🎁

Surprise with Packaging

A gift or thank you card creates loyal customers. First purchase well packaged generates repeat purchases.

⚡

Start with Items You Have

No need to invest at the beginning. Sell clothes you don't wear, unused electronics, books, etc.

📱

Use WhatsApp to Your Advantage

Create catalog on WhatsApp Business, use status to promote and facilitate direct contact.
