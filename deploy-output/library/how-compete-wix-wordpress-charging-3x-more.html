1

Never sell "a website". Sell a complete client acquisition and management system. Websites are commodities, growth systems have premium value.

2

Show ROI, Not Features

Instead of talking about "responsive design", show: "This system brings 10 new clients per month, each spending $50. That's $500 extra every month."

3

4

Offer Results, Not Work

Be the Partner, Not the Supplier

Position yourself as a growth partner. "I'm not selling a website, I'm becoming your digital partner. Your success is my success."
