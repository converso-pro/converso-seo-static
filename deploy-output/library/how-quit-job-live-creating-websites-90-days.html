❌ Common Mistakes (and How to Avoid Them)

1. Quitting too early

Wait until you have at least 2x your current salary for 3 consecutive months before quitting.

2. Charging too cheap

Never charge less than $500 per website. You're delivering a complete solution, not just a website.

3. Not having emergency savings

Have at least 6 months of expenses saved before making the transition.

4. Stop prospecting clients

Even with a full schedule, always reserve time for new clients. The pipeline can never dry up.

5. Not formalizing your business

Register your business as soon as possible. It's simple, cheap, and protects you legally.
