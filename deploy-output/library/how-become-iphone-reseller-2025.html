Complete and updated guide for the global market

📱 Quick Answer

To become an iPhone reseller in 2025, you need:

1) Initial investment of $500 to $1,000
2) Reliable suppliers (importers, national distributors or marketplaces)
3) Management system like Converso.pro to track sales and customers
4) Online and offline sales strategy

With dedication, you can profit $50 to $150 per device and sell 20-30 units per month, generating income of $1,000 to $4,500.

🏪 Where to Find Reliable Suppliers?

1

International Importers

Established companies in Hong Kong, Miami, or Dubai that work with official invoices. Profit margin: 15-25%. Ideal for larger volumes (10+ devices).

2

National Distributors

Wholesalers in major cities. Prices 10-15% higher, but with national warranty and fast delivery. Great for starting out.

3

B2B Marketplaces

Platforms like Alibaba and specialized electronics B2B sites. Purchase security with escrow. Ideal for testing new suppliers.

4

Direct Import

For those with business registration and experience. Buy directly from Asia or USA. Higher profit (30-40%), but requires import knowledge.

5

Reseller Groups

Cooperatives of resellers who buy together for better prices. Entry via referral, excellent networking.

6

Auctions and Pre-owned

iPhones from carriers and companies. Margin can reach 40%, but requires technical knowledge to assess device condition.

🚀 Step by Step to Get Started

1

Define Your Initial Capital

Set aside money for initial investment. Start small: with $500 you can get 1 iPhone 13 or 2 iPhone 11. Reserve 20% for emergencies and working capital.

2

Choose Your Models

iPhone 11, 12 and 13 have best turnover. iPhone 14 and 15 give more profit per unit. Avoid very old models (below X) as they have low demand.

3

Set Up Your Sales System

Create professional profile on Instagram, WhatsApp Business and configure Converso.pro to manage customers, sales and warranties. Automate as much as possible.

4

First Test Purchase

Buy 1-2 units to test the supplier. Check: delivery time, product quality, after-sales support. Only increase volume after validation.

5

Pricing Strategy

Research local competitor prices. Start with 10-15% margin to gain market. After having loyal customers, adjust to 15-20%.

6

Smart Marketing

Daily posts on Instagram, stories with satisfied customers, local WhatsApp groups. Invest $20-50/month in targeted ads.

💡 Tips from Resellers Making $10k+/month

Secrets to Success in Reselling

  • ✅ Offer installments: Up to 3x interest-free via PayPal or similar
  • ✅ Extended warranty: Give 30 days beyond supplier warranty
  • ✅ Express delivery: Same day for sales before 2 PM
  • ✅ Complete kit: Include screen protector and case on purchases above $600
  • ✅ Referral program: $20 discount for referrals
  • ✅ Accept trade-ins: Used iPhones as part of payment
  • ✅ Create urgency: "Last 2 units" always works
  • ✅ Professional photos: Invest in a ring light and neutral background

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a business license to start?

Not mandatory at the beginning. You can start as an individual and formalize when you reach $2,000/month. With a business license you pay less taxes and get better terms with suppliers.

What's the best iPhone to resell in 2025?

iPhone 13 128GB has the best value. Fast turnover (sells in 3-5 days) and margin of $80-120. iPhone 12 is also excellent for price-conscious buyers. iPhone 15 gives more profit, but slower turnover.

How to identify fake iPhone?

Always check IMEI on Apple's website, test Face ID/Touch ID, check if it appears in iTunes, observe finish (fakes have rough edges). When in doubt, only buy from suppliers with proven reputation.

Is it worth selling used/pre-owned iPhone?

Yes! Margin can be higher (30-40%). Market seeking value is huge. Important: be transparent about condition, take detailed photos and offer minimum 30-day warranty.

How to compete with big stores?

Your edge is personalized service, same-day delivery, easy payment terms and better price (you have lower fixed costs). Build customer relationships via WhatsApp and be flexible.
