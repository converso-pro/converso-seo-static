How to Choose the Ideal System for Your Business?

📊 Decision Criteria

1. Booking Volume

  • Up to 50/month: Free options like Converso Free or Setmore
  • 50-200/month: Basic paid plans (Converso Plus, SimplyBook)
  • 200+/month: Professional plans (Booksy, Acuity, Converso Pro)

2. Business Type

  • Salons/Barbershops: Booksy or Converso (marketplace vs own website)
  • Clinics/Medical: Converso or Acuity (patient records vs robustness)
  • Services/Freelancers: Calendly or Converso (meetings vs in-person)
  • Fitness/Studios: Acuity or Converso (classes vs individual)

3. Available Budget

  • $0-10: Converso Free, Cal.com, Setmore Free
  • $10-20: Converso Plus, SimplyBook basic
  • $20-50: Booksy, Square Appointments
  • $50+: Acuity, Calendly Teams, Converso Pro

How to migrate from one system to another?

1. Export data from current system (clients, services) 2. Set up new system 3. Import the data 4. Test for 1 week in parallel 5. Redirect old links 6. Notify clients of the change
